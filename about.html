Heidi Toft

I grew up in a home where we always had a couple of dogs and cats. We adopted some and also took in a couple of stray at times. Until I went away to college, I never knew a home without pets.

After I graduated from college I lived at home for a few years before moving out on my own. I lived for about 10 years without a pet so about 9 years ago, after reading Facebook updates from a friend who fosters dogs, I decided to give it a try. Well, I fostered for about a week and then decided to adopt my first pup. I've fosterd many times since and also failed (adopted my foster pup) so I can't foster anymore. Instead, I volunteer at the cities open-access shelter as well as a couple of no-kill shelters. It's definitely a great way to help our homeless pets find their new forver homes.
